# Yelyp ---> Generates Restaurant Reviews Instantly!
Yelpy is a Next.js app that lets you generate fun, realistic-looking reviews for any restaurant using AI.

## Goal:
- Our plan is to allow people to generate reviews for their very own restaurant!

## Features:
- Enter your restaurant's menu, name, and cuisine.
- Choose positive or negative reviews.
- Generate 1-10 reviews at one time, instantly.

## How to Use:
1. Paste a portion of your menu into the first box.
2. Enter the restaurant name and cuisine.
3. Toggle the sentiment bar to positive or negative.
4. Pick how many reviews you want (1â€“10).
5. Click **Generate Reviews** to see them appear on the right.
